About ZayrahMert

Your trusted online shopping destination in Nepal

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by Dilsad Ansari, ZayrahMert began as a small online store with a vision to provide quality products at affordable prices to customers across Nepal.

What started as a modest venture has now grown into one of Kathmandu's most trusted e-commerce platforms, serving thousands of satisfied customers nationwide.

Our journey has been guided by our core values of integrity, customer satisfaction, and continuous improvement.

Our Mission

At ZayrahMert, we're committed to:

  • Providing genuine, high-quality products
  • Offering competitive prices
  • Ensuring fast and reliable delivery
  • Delivering exceptional customer service
  • Continuously expanding our product range
